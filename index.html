Background

Founded by two college students working together in a dorm room in early 2019, Cold Ink Studios is a startup built in La Jolla, CA. With both founding members working towards degrees from University of California, San Diego. Brent VanZant, an independent game developer serves as Cold Ink's lead programmer and manager. Daniel Mendoza, a incredibly talented composer and music producer is Cold Ink's musical director. Recently, Austin Phlakeo, an awesome software developer has joined the team as a backend engineer. Cold Ink Studios is currently expanding, meaning we're looking for artists and video editors to fill team roles.

Focusing on mobile game development, Cold Ink aims to develop timeless minimilistic games for Android devices. Working in both Unity and GameMaker Studio 2, our team seeks to produce fun and fluid experiences for all our players. At the end of the day, we all love making games and hope to share that passion with you.
